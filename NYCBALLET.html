Ballet is extreme art. I collaborated with Chris Wheeldon (choreographer) and Bright Shane (composer) to help bring Oscar Wilde's tale of love and loss to Lincon Center.

I created a series of hand-painted and computer-generated animations to create a larger than life brush-strokes come to life and a videp-projected garden on stage with plants that grew before your eyes. Watching over the dancers was an animated eye projected onto the moon. Read the review it in the NY Times.
