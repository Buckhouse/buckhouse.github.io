J ames Buckhouse has exhibited at the Whitney Biennial, the Solomon R. Guggenheim’s Works & Process Series, The Institute of Contemporary Art in London, The Berkeley Art Museum, and the Dia Center.  

He has collaborated with leading choreographers at the New York City Ballet, San Francisco Ballet, Oregon Ballet Theatre, and Pennsylvania Ballet to blend the visual and performing arts.  

His novel, All Fashion is Fiction, recieved a tongue-in-cheek genius review in the NY Times.

He regularly lectures on art with past talks at the University of California Berkeley, Stanford University, Harvard University, Parsons, and New York University.
