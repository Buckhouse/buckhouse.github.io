James Buckhouse is an artist practicing at the intersection of the visual and performing arts. He has exhibited at the Whitney Biennial, the Solomon R. Guggenheim’s Works & Process Series, The Institute of Contemporary Art in London, The Berkeley Art Museum, and the Dia Center.
