About

James Buckhouse has exhibited in museums and galleries around the world including the Solomon R. Guggenheim's Works & Process Series, the Whitney Museum of American Art, The Institute of Contemporary Art in London, the Dia Center, and served as a production designer for New York City Ballet, Oregon Ballet Theatre, and Pennsylvania Ballet.

His book, All Fashion is Fiction: the Complete Writings of Serg Riva, was recently reviewed in the NYTimes and is available on Amazon.

He regularly lectures on art and culture with recent lectures at University of California Berkeley, Stanford University, Harvard University, Parsons, and New York University.
