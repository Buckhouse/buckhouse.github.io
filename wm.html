WESTERN MYTHOLOGY

James Buckhouse
For this solo exhibition at the Walter Maciel Gallery in Los Angeles, I travelled to Eastern Oregon to track the wild Kiger mustangs with my father. The trip resulted in drawings, paintings and a large scale video installation.
