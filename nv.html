Created for the 2016 Sensorium at SFBALLET, Nouvelle Vauge is a video curtain that visitors can move through and around. Visitors passed through the waves to enter the theatre, signalling a transformation from normal life to an artistic experience.
SIZE
Two video curtains, each 30' wide, 20' tall
Duration
Video Loop, 4'30"
